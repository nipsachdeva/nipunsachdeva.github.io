How I Work

  • Business-first mindset: I start by understanding the commercial problem — availability, cost, speed, or risk — before choosing any technical solution.
  • End-to-end ownership: From data ingestion and modelling to deployment and stakeholder dashboards.
  • Production over prototypes: I focus on building systems that are reliable, monitored, and used in real-world operations.
  • Automation by default: If a process is repetitive, it should be automated.
